THIS IS WHY WE DO IT

'It's our mission to turn fast food into fast good. We do this by providing plant based meals which are good for humans and the planet.'

FOOD WASTE

One third of all food produced for human consumption is wasted! We believe it's time for a change. That's why we started The Fast Good Company.

 

We are an impact-company that creates 100% plant-based meals and we're on a mission to support the planet. We strive towards operating "waste free". We want to set an example for the entire food industry. Together we can drive positive change for people and the planet. So come join us and let's have some fun along the way.

An estimated 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted globally each year, according to the Food and Agriculture Organization

(FAO) of the United Nations.

FAST GOOD

Taste always comes first. We believe food needs to taste great and fuel your body in a positive way. That's why we create 100% plant-based meals which are loaded with veggies and only contain natural ingredients.

'We will continue until all humans and the
planet are just as cool as our products.'

WHAT WE STAND FOR

QUALITY

We continuously improve the quality and sustainability of our meals and promise to never use processed ingredients, adjectives or refined sugars. We always develop products with a long shelf time.

TRANSPARENCY

We hope to make the food industry a more honest place by being totally transparent in everything we do.

HONESTY

We promise to be a good company which means that our drive to help people and upgrade their lives always come before the reckless pursuit of profit.

SUSTAINABILITY

We strive to be as sustainable and environmentally aware as possible and continue to improve this along our way. We want to do this together with you, if you have great suggestions, ideas you can always let us know.
