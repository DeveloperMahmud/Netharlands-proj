IT'S FROZEN!

We understand that when we mention “frozen food”, you don’t instantly think about how delicious plant-based meals aid our planet in fighting against food waste and global warming as a whole, but rather about bitterballen (famous Dutch snack), cubes of spinach, pizza Hawaii, fish fingers or chocolate fudge brownie…. We are eager to breathe new life into your relationship with the freezer compartment. It’s kind of like the return of record players and Chesterfield sofas. It turns out our frozen plant-based meals hold all their awesome nutritional values (for up to 12 months), meaning you always have a comforting, healthy meals at the go, without having to cook of feeling like a jerk about throwing away food which you forgot about while rushing to make it …..
